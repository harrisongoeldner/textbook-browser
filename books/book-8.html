Introduction to Engineering Analysis

Engineering. Statistics. Mathematics.

Overview

This textbook introduces a systematic problem-solving approach designed specifically for first-year engineering students, helping them transition from basic math and science to rigorous engineering analysis. It covers fundamental principles across key disciplines, including mechanics, electrical circuits, thermodynamics, and fluid mechanics, allowing students to practice the analysis methodology in diverse contexts. The text also emphasizes essential skills such as data analysis, statistics, and the use of computer tools, providing a solid foundation for more advanced coursework. By focusing on the logical process of solving analytical problems, it prepares students for the challenges of the engineering curriculum.
