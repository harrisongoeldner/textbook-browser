The Curious Writer

Writing. Research. Academic.

Overview

This rhetoric and writing guide prioritizes inquiry as the central engine of the writing process, encouraging students to begin with questions rather than answers. It bridges the gap between personal and academic writing by treating composition as a method of discovery rather than just a formulaic procedure. The text guides readers through diverse genres—including personal essays, profiles, and arguments—while emphasizing research and revision as organic components of learning. By fostering a "curious" mindset, it aims to help students develop the critical thinking skills necessary for effective academic communication.
