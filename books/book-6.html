Database Processing

Fundamentals. Design. Implementation.

Overview

This textbook provides a thorough introduction to the core principles of database management systems, balancing theoretical foundations with practical application. It guides readers through the entire database lifecycle, covering essential topics such as the relational model, normalization, and SQL programming. The text emphasizes real-world skills through a unique "spiral approach," where concepts are revisited and applied in increasingly complex projects. Recent editions also explore contemporary trends like Big Data, cloud computing, and NoSQL, ensuring students are prepared for the modern digital workplace.
